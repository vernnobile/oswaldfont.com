Oswald is a Free and Open font family.

Oswald is a reworking of the classic 'Gothic' and 'Grotesque' type styles, historically most usually represented by the late Nineteenth and early Twentieth Century faces such as;
'Alternate Gothic'
'Franklyn Gothic'
'Headline Gothic'
'Grotesque No. 33'

Oswald is designed to be used freely across the internet by web browsers on desktop computers, laptops and mobile devices.

The Oswald fonts are available from Google Fonts and Font Squirrel in a variety of weights and versions.

Oswald is under active development. More weights and versions are in the pipeline, and improvements to the allready in-use fonts are regularly made.

Check out the Oswald repo at github where you can download the latest fonts and / or download the source designs.
